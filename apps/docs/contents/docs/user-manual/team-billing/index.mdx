## Team access

The platform already models access at two levels:

- platform-scoped permissions for the internal owner workspace
- shop-scoped roles and permissions for day-to-day tenant operations

This matters in practice because operational pages, exports, and member management are not just
UI toggles. They map to stable capability checks in the backend.

## Subscription behavior

- Trialing, active, and overdue subscriptions currently keep the operational workspace available.
- Expired, cancelled, and inactive states block plan-gated features.
- Billing and recovery surfaces stay reachable so a shop can inspect status and restore access.

## Why this matters for documentation

Operator documentation needs to explain not only what a page does, but also why a user may or may
not be able to access it.

- `team.members` affects member management routes.
- export access depends on the relevant plan items.
- shop billing pages are part of the operational recovery path.

<Note type="warning" title="Implementation detail">
  Feature access is enforced through stable feature codes, so display labels can change over time
  without breaking authorization logic.
</Note>
