FAQ

Common questions

Does it connect to Facebook / Messenger automatically? +
No — and that's intentional. Connecting to Facebook's API is complex and slow to approve. Instead, you copy the order info from Messenger and paste it into the app. It takes about 10 seconds and you stay in full control.
Facebook API နှင့် တိုက်ရိုက်ချိတ်မထားပါ — ရည်ရွယ်ချက်ရှိရှိ ဒီဇိုင်းဆွဲထားပါသည်
Is my data safe? What happens if I stop paying? +
Your data is stored securely and never sold. If you cancel your subscription, you have 30 days to export all your orders and customer data before the account is closed.
သင်၏ data များ လုံခြုံစွာ သိမ်းဆည်းထားမည်
How do I pay? Do you accept KBZ Pay / Wave Money? +
Yes — we accept KBZ Pay, Wave Money, and AYA Pay. After subscribing, just message us on Facebook with your payment screenshot and we'll activate your account within 1 hour.
KBZ Pay, Wave Money, AYA Pay ဖြင့် ပေးချေနိုင်သည်
Can I use it on both Android and iPhone? +
Yes. Order Manager works on Android and iOS. You can also use it from any web browser on your phone or computer — no installation needed for the web version.
